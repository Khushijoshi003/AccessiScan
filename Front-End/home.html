AccessiScan

Your website's accessibility made simple and fast. We analyze your website's accessibility to ensure it meets the necessary standards and provides an improved experience for all users, including those with disabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AccessiScan?

AccessiScan is a tool that helps identify accessibility issues on your website, making it more user-friendly for everyone.

Why should I use it?

To meet compliance standards and ensure your content is accessible to users with disabilities.

How does it work?

Simply enter the URL of your website, and AccessiScan will analyze the page for any accessibility issues. It provides feedback on potential improvements and solutions.

Is AccessiScan free to use?

Yes, AccessiScan offers a free version with basic accessibility checks. For advanced features, a premium version is available.
